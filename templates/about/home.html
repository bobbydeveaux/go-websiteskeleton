About Us

Experience Counts

DVO Media are experts in our field, this is largely due to the extensive experience of our developers. One of the Directors, Bobby, has been coding since he was 9 years old. Working for a variety of corporate and bluechip companies, as well as smaller web development studios and in house development teams, Bobby has seen it all. During this time he has mentored, coached and managed development teams, and now has a keen eye for talent which is immensly useful when recruiting new developers. The talent at DVO Media is second to none, and no one is afraid to introduce new technology and push the boundaries even further.

What can we do for you?

At DVO Media we offer wide range of services ranging from bespoke software development, right through to onsite development and coaching. We can also manage your servers, migrate services into the cloud, improve server security and even performance benchmark you sites. Whatever you require, get in touch and we can give you a personalised quote.
